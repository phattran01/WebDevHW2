My Hobby: Art!

Art has always been my favorite hobby ever since I was young. There was never a day where my creativity had a break. It was my only talent; drawing. I loved being able to show my imagination on paper.

The image on the left is one of my favorite pieces I've done this year. It was during summertime and I remember asking a person I met recently what their favorite flower was. She said sunflowers and this red Chinese lantern flower. I had the time of my life drawing it. I remembered not taking a break when I drew it, because I was so determined to finish it for her.
