"Break My Heart Again" by FINNEAS

Transcript

Alright, so, my favorite video, or music video, is FINNEAS "Break My Heart Again." One of the things I really like about this little piece is the symbolism on his shirt. In the video, you could see how he struggles to take off his shirt but he doesn't, and if you compare it with the song itself and the lyrics, I feel like the shirt represents his relationship with his girl and how he can't let go of her even though she hurts him a lot.
